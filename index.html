Simple Recipe to Prepare a Basic Cup of Tea

Ingredients:

  • 1 tea bag or 1 teaspoon of loose tea leaves (black tea, green tea, or any tea of your choice)
  • 1 cup of water (about 240 ml)
  • Optional: Milk, sugar, honey, lemon, or spices (like ginger or cardamom)

Instructions:

  1. Boil the Water:
    • Heat 1 cup of water in a kettle or on the stove until it reaches a rolling boil. The temperature varies depending on the type of tea:
      • For black tea, use water just off a rolling boil (about 100°C / 212°F).
      • For green tea, use water around 70–80°C (160–175°F), just below boiling.
  2. Add the Tea:
    • If using a tea bag, place it into your cup.
    • If using loose tea leaves, measure out about 1 teaspoon of tea and place it in a tea infuser or directly into the cup.
  3. Pour Water Over the Tea:
    • Pour the hot water over the tea bag or leaves, ensuring that they are fully submerged.
  4. Steep the Tea:
    • Let the tea steep for the right amount of time:
      • For black tea, steep for 3-5 minutes.
      • For green tea, steep for 2-3 minutes.
    • Adjust steeping time based on your taste preference: a longer steep will make the tea stronger and more robust.
  5. Remove the Tea Bag or Strain the Tea:
    • If you're using a tea bag, remove it from the cup after steeping.
    • If you're using loose leaves, strain the tea into another cup.
  6. Add Optional Ingredients:
    • Milk: Add a splash of milk for a creamy texture, especially if using black tea.
    • Sweeteners: Add sugar or honey if you prefer sweet tea.
    • Lemon: A slice of lemon can give your tea a refreshing citrusy flavor.
    • Spices: You can add a pinch of ground ginger, cardamom, or cinnamon for extra warmth and flavor.
  7. Stir and Enjoy:
    • Stir your tea, taste it, and adjust sweetness or flavorings as desired.

Now your cup of tea is ready to enjoy!
